About the Alliance

InfraGard Middle Tennessee Members Alliance is a nonprofit, public-private partnership with the FBI dedicated to the protection of U.S. critical infrastructure.

With more than 350 active members as of 2026, the Alliance connects private sector professionals, academics, and government officials with law enforcement to share actionable intelligence, deliver training on emerging security threats, and strengthen regional resilience across all 16 critical infrastructure sectors.

Our chapter serves the Middle Tennessee region with a commitment to trust, rigor, and mission-driven collaboration. Meetings are confidential, candid, and designed to deliver real value to members and the communities they protect.

Membership

InfraGard membership is open to U.S. citizens who work in or support critical infrastructure sectors. The application process is managed nationally through InfraGard.org and coordinated locally by the FBI Nashville Field Office.

Membership is not automatic. All applicants go through a structured vetting process to ensure the integrity and security of the network.

  • Federal background investigation
  • Identity verification via ID.me
  • Review and approval by the local FBI InfraGard coordinator

Applications are submitted through the national InfraGard portal. The process typically takes several weeks from submission to approval.

Apply at InfraGard.org

Already a member? Your chapter affiliation can be updated through the same portal.
